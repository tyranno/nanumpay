## Full Description (under 4000 characters)

### What is NanumPay?

NanumPay is a commission management system for insurance sales representatives.
Check your performance in real-time and view your commission payment history.

### Key Features

**For Sales Representatives**
- View personal performance
- Check commission payment history
- Grade-based payment status
- View organizational hierarchy

**For Planners**
- Commission history lookup
- Monthly commission overview
- Managed representatives status

**For Administrators**
- Register and manage representatives
- Organizational chart management
- Commission ledger management
- Planner management
- System settings

### Features

- Easy login
- Real-time data access
- Intuitive UI/UX
- Secure personal data protection

### Usage Information

This app is exclusively for Nanum Asset employees and affiliated planners.
For registration and inquiries, please contact the company.
